About Me

I am an 11th grade student from San Francisco, California, and I currently attend Phillips Academy. I am fascinated by space and engineering and I hope to pursue a career in aerospace engineering. I enjoy challenging myself to learn new skills and expand the horizons of my abilities. I also love playing sports and staying active. I play soccer, volleyball, and I ski. I love combining my passion for making and sports through projects like my climbing wall or bike build.

Experiences/Organizations

ACES

I am President of Andover Competitive Engineering Society, a team competing in the Real World Design Challenge and the F1 in Schools competition.

Astronomy Club

As a Co-President, I organize and lead meetings and observing open houses in the Phillips Academy Observatory.

Divest Andover

I am a member of Divest Andover, a student-run coalition advocating for Phillips Academy's divestment from fossil fuels.

Student Leadership

As a dorm proctor and member of the Andover Admissions Ambassadors, I am responsible for assisting the house counseling team and the admissions department.

Systems Admin/Technician

I have worked for 4 years in the Marin Country Day School Tech Department, assisting in hardware/software setup/support and network upgrades.

Summer Camp Experience

I was the youngest Junior Counselor at "The X Kids" and a counselor at "Wonderlab," maker-oriented camps promoting STEAM education.
