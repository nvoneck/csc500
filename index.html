About Me

I am an 11th grade student from San Francisco, California and I currently attend Phillips Academy. I am fascinated with space and engineering, and hope to pursue a career in aerospace engineering. I enjoy challenging myself to learn new skills and expand the horizons of my abilities. I also love playing sports and staying active. I play soccer, volleyball, and I ski. I love combining my love of making and sports with projects like my climbing wall or bike.
